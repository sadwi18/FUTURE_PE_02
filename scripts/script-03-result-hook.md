# Script 03 — Result Hook
**Brand:** Flowlog  
**Hook type:** Claim / Social proof  
**Tone:** Proud user (understated, not hype)  
**Platform:** Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts  
**Estimated read time:** 20 seconds


## Instagram Reels Version

**[HOOK]**  
"January was my best revenue month ever. The only thing I changed was this."

**[BODY]**  
I didn't hire anyone. Didn't work more hours. I started using Flowlog — one workspace for my whole day. Tasks, time, client work, all in one place. 
Stopped losing an hour every morning deciding what to work on. Stopped ending the day not sure what I'd actually done. It sounds small. It's not small.

**[CTA]**  
"14 days free. Link in bio."

**[VISUAL DIRECTION]**  
Creator looking into camera, calm confidence. No hype energy. Simple background — home office or coffee shop. Short, punchy delivery. No filler expressions.



## YouTube Shorts Version

**[HOOK — on-screen text overlay]**  
*"Best month ever. One change."*

**[HOOK — spoken]**  
"January was my highest-revenue month since I went freelance. I didn't change my rates. Didn't land some big client. 
The only thing I actually changed was my daily workflow."

**[BODY]**  
I started using Flowlog. It's a single workspace — your tasks, your schedule, your client notes, your time tracking — all one screen. 
I know that sounds basic. But the reason it worked is because I stopped spending 45 minutes every morning deciding what to do and where to put things. 
I stopped ending the day wondering what I actually completed. When you're running a one-person business, your attention is the product. 
Anything that fragments it is costing you money. Flowlog stopped the fragmentation. That's it. That's the whole reason.

**[CTA]**  
"Free trial, link in the description. No credit card. See if one week changes how your days feel."

**[THUMBNAIL TEXT SUGGESTION]**  
*"BEST MONTH. ONE CHANGE."*

**[VISUAL DIRECTION]**  
Creator seated, relaxed, direct eye contact. Show a quick phone/screen glimpse of a clean Flowlog dashboard mid-video.
End on creator face — no product logo close-up needed.
