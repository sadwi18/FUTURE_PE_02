# Script 04 — Contrarian Hook
**Brand:** Flowlog  
**Hook type:** Identity / Pattern interrupt  
**Tone:** Casual expert (slightly provocative)  
**Platform:** Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts  
**Estimated read time:** 28 seconds


## Instagram Reels Version

**[HOOK]**  
"Stop building a productivity system. You don't need a system. You need to stop switching tabs."

**[BODY]**  
I spent two years optimizing my Notion setup. Databases, linked views, templates — genuinely beautiful. Completely useless. 
Because I was organizing instead of working. The real problem was never structure. It was context-switching. Every time I moved between apps, I lost the thread. 
Flowlog fixed that. One screen. Everything. I stopped touching my Notion the day I started using it.

**[CTA]**  
"Free trial. Link in bio. Don't build another system — just try this for a week."

**[VISUAL DIRECTION]**  
Creator slightly animated — this one has a bit of "I have a take" energy. Can gesture toward camera. 
Natural, candid, not staged. Kitchen or home office background.


## YouTube Shorts Version

**[HOOK — on-screen text overlay]**  
*"Your productivity system is the problem."*

**[HOOK — spoken]**  
"Your productivity system is not helping you. It might actually be the thing slowing you down."

**[BODY]**  
I know that's annoying to hear if you've spent weeks building one. I did too. Two years of Notion templates, GTD frameworks, second brain setups. 
And I was organized. Beautifully organized. I was just never actually in flow. Because the system required constant maintenance. 
Every morning I was feeding the system instead of doing the work. The insight that changed things: I didn't need more structure. I needed less switching.
Flowlog is one screen. Plan your day, list your tasks, track your time, store your client notes. That's all it does. It doesn't want to be your second brain.
It just wants to make today less chaotic. And honestly? That's all I ever actually needed.

**[CTA]**  
"Free 14-day trial in the description. If your current system is working perfectly, ignore this. 
But if you rebuild it every three months — maybe try something different."

**[THUMBNAIL TEXT SUGGESTION]**  
*"YOUR SYSTEM IS THE PROBLEM."*

**[VISUAL DIRECTION]**  
More expressive delivery — creator can lean forward on the contrarian hook. Mid-video settle into a calmer storytelling mode. 
End with a half-smile, not a sales grin. Feels like advice from a friend, not a pitch.
