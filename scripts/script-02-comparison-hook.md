# Script 02 — Comparison Hook
**Brand:** Flowlog  
**Hook type:** Claim  
**Tone:** Skeptical convert  
**Platform:** Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts  
**Estimated read time:** 22 seconds


## Instagram Reels Version

**[HOOK]**  
"I cancelled five subscriptions in one week. Didn't miss any of them."

**[BODY]**  
Notion. Trello. Toggl. Calendly's scheduling add-on. A random habit tracker. All gone. 
Not because I gave up on being organized — because Flowlog does all of it in one place, and honestly does it better for how my brain works.
It's built for people running a one-person business, not for teams with project managers. I resisted switching because I'd spent months building my Notion setup. 
But I was spending more time in the setup than in the work. Switched to Flowlog on a Monday. By Wednesday I felt different.

**[CTA]**  
"Free trial, link in bio. Try it for one week and tell me I'm wrong."

**[VISUAL DIRECTION]**  
Creator cancelling app subscriptions on their phone screen — genuine on-camera reaction. 
Cut to them at their desk looking calm and focused. Conversational, slightly smug energy (in a likeable way).



## YouTube Shorts Version

**[HOOK — on-screen text overlay]**  
*"I cancelled 5 subscriptions."*

**[HOOK — spoken]**  
"I cancelled five app subscriptions in one week. Notion, Trello, Toggl, two others. And I'm more organized now than I ever was with all of them."

**[BODY]**  
This isn't an ad for minimalism. I genuinely needed all those tools — or I thought I did. The problem was the switching. 
Every time I went from Trello to Notion to my timer and back, I lost 5–10 minutes of mental momentum. Multiply that by a workday and you're losing hours.
Flowlog is built for one-person businesses. It's not trying to be an enterprise project management tool. 
It's just: here's your day, here are your tasks, here's your time, here are your client notes. Done. 
I stopped rebuilding my system every month and started actually finishing things. 
That's not nothing — that's kind of everything when you're the only person on your team.

**[CTA]**  
"Free 14-day trial in the description. No card required. Try it before you spend another month inside a Notion template you didn't finish."

**[THUMBNAIL TEXT SUGGESTION]**  
*"CANCELLED 5 APPS. UPGRADED."*

**[VISUAL DIRECTION]**  
Open with creator on phone cancelling subscriptions. Mid-video, switch to laptop view. Close on creator speaking directly to camera, relaxed. 
No B-roll music bed — silence or ambient sound only.
