# Hook Generator Prompt

Use this prompt to generate 10+ unique hooks for any single product.
Hooks are the 0–3 second opening of a UGC video. They decide if someone watches or scrolls.


## The Prompt
You are a scroll-stopping hook writer for short-form video ads.
A hook must do ONE of these things in under 10 words:

Name a pain the viewer quietly lives with
Say something they've never heard before
Make a specific claim that feels almost too good to be real
Start mid-story so they feel like they walked into something already happening
Call out exactly who this is for (so everyone else self-selects out)

Product: [product name and one-line description]
Target viewer: [specific person — job, frustration, daily behaviour]
Generate 12 hooks. Organize them by type:

3 x Pain hooks (naming a frustration)
3 x Claim hooks (bold, specific result)
3 x Story hooks (mid-action opening)
3 x Identity hooks (calling out the specific viewer)

Rules:

No hook should start with "Are you..."
No hook should use the product name
No hook should use the words: game-changer, hack, secret, tips
Every hook must be under 12 words
Write like a real person, not a copywriter


## Example Output (Flowlog)

**Pain Hooks**
- "I had 11 tabs open and still missed a client deadline."
- "Notion was supposed to fix my chaos. It became the chaos."
- "I was working 10-hour days and somehow falling behind."

**Claim Hooks**
- "I got back 90 minutes a day and I didn't change my hours."
- "One app replaced five. My mornings are unrecognizable now."
- "I finished more in 4 hours than I used to in 8."

**Story Hooks**
- "So I'm on a client call and I literally cannot find the brief—"
- "Third time this week I invoiced the wrong amount."
- "My to-do list had 47 items. Zero of them were priorities."

**Identity Hooks**
- "If you're a freelancer running your business on vibes and tabs—"
- "Solo founder with no team and no system — this one's for you."
- "If your productivity app is making you less productive--"
