# Hook Bank — 12 Scroll-Stopping Hooks for Flowlog

Mix and match these with any script. Every hook is under 12 words.  
Organized by type. None use the product name.


## Pain Hooks
*(Name a frustration the viewer quietly lives with)*

1. "I had 9 tabs open at 11pm and still got nothing done."
2. "Notion was supposed to fix my chaos. It became the chaos."
3. "I was working 10-hour days and somehow always falling behind."
4. "Every morning I'd spend an hour deciding what to work on."
5. "I kept rebuilding my productivity setup instead of doing the work."


## Claim Hooks
*(Bold, specific result — feels almost too real)*

6. "I cancelled five subscriptions in one week. Didn't miss any of them."
7. "January was my best revenue month ever. One thing changed."
8. "I got back 90 minutes a day without changing my work hours."
9. "I finished my whole to-do list before noon. That's never happened."


## Story Hooks
*(Drop the viewer mid-scene)*

10. "So I'm on a client call and I literally cannot find the brief—"
11. "Third time this week I forgot to log my hours until midnight."
12. "I opened Trello, forgot why. Opened Notion, forgot that too."


## Identity Hooks
*(Call out exactly who this is for)*

*Bonus 3 — use as openers before any script*

- "If you're a freelancer running your business on tabs and anxiety—"
- "Solo founder, no team, doing everything yourself — this is for you."
- "If your productivity app is making you less productive, stay for 20 seconds."


**Usage tip:** Pair a Pain Hook with Script 01. Pair a Claim Hook with Script 03. Pair the Identity Hook with Script 04 for maximum resonance.
