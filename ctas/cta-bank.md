#  CTA Bank — 10 Calls-to-Action for Flowlog

Three styles: Soft, Hard, and Curiosity. Choose based on script tone.


## Soft CTAs
*(Low pressure — works best when the script already built strong trust)*

1. "There's a free trial. Link in bio if you want to check it out."
2. "14 days free, no card. See if it helps your mornings."
3. "I'll drop the link. Don't sign up — just look at it for 2 minutes."
4. "Link in the description. It's free to try, weirdly low stakes."


## Hard CTAs
*(Direct — works when the script ends on a strong result or proof point)*

5. "Go try it. 14 days free, link in bio."
6. "Free trial, no credit card. Link in bio right now."
7. "Link in description. Stop the tab chaos today."


## Curiosity CTAs
*(Make them click to understand — works with contrarian or story scripts)*

8. "I'll link it. It's not what you think a productivity app looks like."
9. "Check the link in bio. The interface alone might change how you think about your day."
10. "One week. That's all it took for me. Link in bio."

**Platform notes:**  
- Instagram Reels → "link in bio"  
- YouTube Shorts → "link in the description" or "pinned in comments"  
- Never say "click here" on video — say "go to" or "check"
